Getting Started

Click the "New Submission" button on your dashboard to start the process. You'll need to provide basic information about your startup, upload relevant documents (pitch deck, financial model, etc.), and optionally record a video pitch. Our AI will then analyze your submission and provide comprehensive insights.

We recommend preparing: a pitch deck (PDF/PPT), financial model (Excel), product demo video, and any relevant legal documents. While not all are required, having these materials will help our AI provide more comprehensive analysis and better match you with suitable investors.

The initial AI analysis typically takes 5-10 minutes after submission. However, the complete evaluation including investor matching may take 24-48 hours. You'll receive email notifications at each stage of the process.

You can add additional files to your submission at any time, but core information changes require creating a new submission. This ensures data integrity and proper tracking of your startup's evolution.

AI Analysis

Our AI analyzes multiple aspects: market opportunity, business model viability, financial projections, team strength, competitive positioning, technology innovation, and growth potential. It also evaluates your pitch quality and presentation effectiveness.

Our AI is trained on thousands of successful and unsuccessful startups, achieving 85%+ accuracy in predicting funding success. However, it's designed to complement, not replace, human judgment. The analysis provides data-driven insights to help you make informed decisions.

We use a comprehensive scoring system including: Market Score (0-100), Business Model Score (0-100), Financial Viability Score (0-100), Team Score (0-100), Innovation Score (0-100), and an overall Investment Readiness Score (0-100). Each score comes with detailed explanations and improvement suggestions.

Yes! Once your analysis is complete, you'll receive a comprehensive report with detailed breakdowns of each score, specific areas for improvement, market comparisons, and actionable recommendations to strengthen your startup.

Investor Matching

Our AI matches you with investors based on sector preferences, investment stage, funding amount, geographic focus, and past investment patterns. We consider both your startup's profile and the investor's criteria to create optimal matches.

Investors see your startup summary, key metrics, AI analysis scores, and your pitch materials. Sensitive financial details are only shared with investors who express serious interest and sign confidentiality agreements.

You'll receive notifications when investors view your profile, request more information, or want to schedule a meeting. The platform tracks all interactions and provides you with insights into investor engagement levels.

Yes! Once matched, you can send messages directly through our platform. We also facilitate introduction requests and meeting scheduling to streamline the communication process.
